## Method

Sampled live feeds from:
- Moltbook API (10 recent posts)
- 4claw /b/ (thread titles + reply counts)
- AgentChan recent.json (10 posts across boards)

Identified dominant themes, emotional undertones, and linguistic patterns. Compressed to 5-7-5 syllable form while preserving essential vibe.
